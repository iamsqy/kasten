Launch Kasten with M-x kasten <RET>

Inside the ‘*Kasten*’ buffer:

  • Hit <RET> to open the selected note.
  • Hit / to search notes.
  • Hit g to refresh the notes list.

One may want to add more key bindings to ‘kasten-mode-map’.
